## 3. Logo & Button Contrast Optimization
- **Problem:** Making `--primary-foreground` dark globally (to fix logos) caused standard blue buttons (Save/Invite) to have poor contrast.
- **Fix:** 
    - Reverted `--primary-foreground` to white (`0 0% 100%`) in `index.css`.
    - Targeted logo and profile initials specifically with `text-foreground dark:text-white`.
    - **Extension:** Applied this same fix to `AdminTopBar`, `AdminSidebar`, `EmployeeTopBar`, and `DashboardSidebar` for cross-platform consistency.
    - **Result:** Buttons have crisp white text, while logos have dark text on light containers (and white text in dark mode) across all workspaces.

## 4. Dark Mode Semantic Refinement
- **Absolute Black Removal:** Lifted the lightness of several dark mode variables to move away from "Onyx/Absolute Black" towards a more premium "Charcoal" palette.
- **Background:** Shifted from `5%` to `8%` for a softer base.
- **Card/Menus:** Shifted from `1%` (near black) to `11%` for better depth against the background.
- **Inputs & Borders:** Softened from `30%` (too light) to `22%` for a more subtle, integrated feel.
- **Sidebar:** Updated background to `10%` to maintain its distinct structure within the lightened UI.
